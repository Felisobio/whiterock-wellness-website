**1. Cat-Cow Stretch**
Start on all fours with your hands under your shoulders and knees under your hips. Slowly arch your back upward like a cat, hold for 5 seconds, then lower your belly and lift your head like a cow. Repeat 10 times.

**2. Child's Pose**
From all fours, sit your hips back toward your heels and stretch your arms forward along the floor. Hold for 30 seconds. This gently decompresses the lower spine.

**3. Knee-to-Chest Stretch**
Lie on your back with knees bent. Pull one knee gently toward your chest and hold for 20 seconds. Switch sides. This releases tension in the lower back and hips.

**4. Seated Spinal Twist**
Sit upright in a chair. Cross your right leg over your left and gently twist your upper body to the right, placing your left hand on your right knee. Hold 20 seconds each side.

**5. Pelvic Tilt**
Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat. Gently flatten your lower back against the floor by tightening your abdominal muscles. Hold for 10 seconds. Repeat 10 times.
