Meet the Doctor

Trained where precision
meets tradition.

Dr. Paul Johnson Chima's journey into chiropractic began in China — where he immersed himself in one of the world's oldest and most rigorous traditions of spinal medicine. At his Chinese chiropractic institution, he mastered techniques that combine ancient Eastern bodywork with modern biomechanical science.

Returning to Nigeria with that rare Eastern-trained precision, Dr. Paul founded WhiteRock Wellness with one mission: to make world-class chiropractic care accessible to everyday Lagosians. No gimmicks. No one-size-fits-all. Just careful, personalised care that gets to the root of your pain.
